About L3 

Genome analysis gives researchers better insights on the genetic bases of drug response and disease. These findings can make medicine more effective.

Right now, the world is sequencing genomes faster than it can be analysed. That means missed opportunities in saving lives. L3 wants to change that.

L3 Bioinformatics aims to provide solutions that make working with genomic data fast, accurate and easy. Genomics is revolutionising healthcare, and L3 is inventing and developing its future.

L3 is based in Hong Kong and specialises in algorithm research and development, application software development, IT infrastructure design and implementation, and NGS Analysis.
